What is FIBER OPTIC CABLE

A fiber optic cable is a network cable containing thin strands of glass fibers inside a protective casing. These fibers transmit data as light signals, offering higher bandwidth and longer transmission distances compared to traditional copper cables. They are commonly used for internet, telecommunications, and high-speed data networks.
